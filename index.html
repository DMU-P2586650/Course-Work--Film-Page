Synopsis

Have you ever felt like there was someone watching you? In the corner of your eye you catch a glimpse of something not quite right, somthing off? A shiver up your spine you cant explain? It all has a reason, a purpose. Dana, Curt, Jules, Marty and Holden find this out when their trip to a cabin in the woods isnt the quiet party get away they had hoped for. Something sinister, even corporate, is watching them influencing their actions as the night goes on.
